BrainBolt - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how BrainBolt ("we", "our") handles data in connection with our website, YouTube channel, and TikTok account (@BrainBoltDaily), and our use of the TikTok Content Posting API and YouTube Data API.

2. Data We Access

BrainBolt's application connects exclusively to our own YouTube and TikTok developer accounts. Through these API integrations, we access:

  • Upload status and video metadata of videos we publish ourselves (to confirm successful publishing).
  • Basic profile information of our own connected account (e.g. account ID), required by the platform's OAuth flow.

We do not collect, access, or store data belonging to any other TikTok or YouTube user. We do not request or process viewer personal data through these APIs.

3. Scopes Requested (TikTok)

  • video.upload — used to upload our pre-produced video files to TikTok's media library.
  • video.publish — used to publish the uploaded video as a post on our own connected account.

These scopes are used solely to automate publishing of our own original content. No data obtained through these scopes is shared with third parties, sold, or used for advertising.

4. Website Data

Our website (this page) does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or collect any personal information from visitors.

5. Data Retention

Authentication tokens used to publish content are stored securely and only for as long as needed to operate the publishing pipeline. They can be revoked at any time by disconnecting the app in the platform's developer/account settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data obtained via platform APIs with third parties.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us via our YouTube or TikTok channel: @BrainBoltDaily.
